My Story

A Journey from Code to C-Suite

I started as a computer science student who loved building things. Eighteen years later, I've led research organizations at OKX, Twitter, and Yahoo, earned an MBA, and I still build — now with AI as my co-pilot. My path has been anything but linear, and that's exactly what makes it work.

The Story

An Uncommon Path

The Engineer

It started with a CS degree and a love for building. As a full-stack developer, I learned to think in systems — how pieces connect, where things break, and why elegant solutions matter.

The Pivot

I moved from building products to understanding the people who use them. Market research at Unilever and Clorox taught me how to translate human behavior into business strategy — and an MBA sharpened the business lens.

The Research Leader

At Twitter, Yahoo, and OKX, I built research organizations from scratch. Not just teams — entire functions. I operated from the CEO office at OKX, shaping product direction across fintech, social media, and crypto. Global teams across APAC, Europe, and the US.

The Builder Returns

Now I'm doing what I've always done best — building. AI agents, shipped products, end-to-end prototypes. The engineer and the researcher have finally merged.
